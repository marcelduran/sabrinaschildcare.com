Welcome Families

At Sabrina's Child Care we believe that it is our responsibility as caregivers and educators to create an environment where children are encouraged to play and to solve problems as they develop cognitive, motor and language skills. We also nurture them socially, emotionally and physically so they are confident and happy. We believe that positive reinforcement is important for children to develop self-esteem. Activities designed to stimulate their senses and natural curiosity, expand on their language development and understanding, and build their fine and gross motor skills will be offered daily.

It is our goal to keep your child safe and provide all of our children with the best possible education delivered by motivated, inspirational teachers. We provide children with the tools and resources necessary to explore and discover the world around them while having fun every day. Through hands-on activities, encouragment, exploration and learning, Sabrina's Child Care supports each child's differing abilities. Children explore music and movement, math, science, art, language and much more.

We provide high quality home ChildCare and we welcome you to be part of our family.
